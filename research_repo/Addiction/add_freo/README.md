Notes for this influence:

Addiction does not directly influence Freedom of Speech.

It can, however, severely influence the ability to actually speak. A good illustration appears in *The Wolf of Wall Street*, during the "Quaalude scene", when the characters take “lemons” and can not articulate their words. From their perspective they are speaking perfectly clearly; to everyone else it sounds like complete nonsense.

This is a biochemical impairment, not a constitutional one.
