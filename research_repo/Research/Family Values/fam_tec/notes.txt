Notes for this influence:

Family Values -> Technology = +0.15

Family values mediates how technology is mediated and integrated by children. Parents guide children's digital learning, well-being, and technological literacy. Technology can strengthen family values but in some cases can degrade them when adopted in excess. 

It can reduce face-to-face communication and attention to social dynamics. This is a reverse asymmetric influence from technology to Family values which is negative.
