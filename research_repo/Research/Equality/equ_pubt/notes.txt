Notes for this influence:

Equality -> Public Trust = +0.12

Equality has a direct positive effect on public trust. When people perceive higher levels of equality, particularly economic fairness, equal opportunity, and non-discriminatory treatment, trust in social environments and public institutions tends to increase. These forms of equality reduce feelings of exclusion and arbitrariness, strengthening confidence that systems operate fairly and predictably. The resulting association with public trust is consistent, though moderate in magnitude.
