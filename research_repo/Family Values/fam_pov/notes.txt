Notes for this influence:

Family Values -> Poverty =  0

I am very skeptical of this value. I can rationalize a negative influence, that family values reduces poverty, however, the research indicates that this happens through indirect pathways rather than a direct causal relationship. Increasing family values doesn't directly mean less poverty. I want to set this slightly lower than zero, maybe around -0.03 but I can not justify it as a direct influence.

The research suggests the mechanism would be something like this:
Family Values
   ↓
Family Structure (marriage stability, cohabitation)
   ↓
Employment patterns / household size / welfare fit
   ↓
Poverty
